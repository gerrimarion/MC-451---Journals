## Your Reflection

I hadn't really thought about it until reading Chapter 8 on "Music Immersion," but I have started to notice things the more I "immerse" myself with my project. 

For starters, I had to switch around my controlled group of 10 men and 10 women to 5/5 because of things I noticed. The first time, when I thought I had the complete dataset, it was after looking through "100 Days in Stardew Valley" videos on YouTube and seeing how much content was there. After going through each video one by one, I already started to find things that made me need to shift some stuff around, but also gave me a head start with my project.

When searching for "100 Days in Stardew Valley," there was a mix of things in there. Men were mostly the ones who had never played SDV or only played for an hour at most, or what would be roughly a week in SDV time. Very few women had never or rarely played SDV for the first time, though I did find some. Most women's versions were adding a challenge element to the "100 Days in Stardew Valley" videos: some playing it as it is in a speed-run way, others playing as different types of farmers (miner, rancher, fisherman, etc.), 100 translating to "100%" the game for the "Perfection" Achievement, and some played with exclusions, such as "BUT I have a tiny farm," which I later learned is a mod to make your farm land smaller. 

I gave everything a first once-over, then I started to notice things the more digging I did. It's also helped me figure out the theory I'm trying to use, the questions I'm trying to ask, and the things I want to learn more about.
